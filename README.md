### Ecxo Games

As **Co-Founder** of [Ecxo Games](https://ecxogames.ca), I'm building the future of interactive entertainment. Ecxo Games is a game development studio based in Montreal, Canada, focused on creating innovative and engaging gaming experiences that push the boundaries of creativity and technology.

**What We Do:**
- Develop original games across multiple platforms
- Create immersive worlds with cutting-edge design
- Leverage modern game engines (Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot)
- Integrate custom audio and visual effects
- Foster innovation in gameplay mechanics and storytelling

Our mission is to deliver unforgettable gaming experiences that resonate with players worldwide. From concept to launch, we're committed to quality, creativity, and pushing the limits of what games can be.

Visit [ecxogames.ca](https://ecxogames.ca) to learn more about our projects and upcoming releases!
